## 🎓 Messaging Mistakes to Avoid

### **❌ Don't Say This**

1. "Advanced Security Platform" 
   - Too vague, everyone says this
   
2. "Enterprise-Grade"
   - Marketing buzzword, means nothing
   
3. "Industry-Leading"
   - Unsubstantiated claim

4. "Best-in-Class"
   - Empty superlative

5. "Cutting-Edge Technology"
   - Too generic

### **✅ Say This Instead**

1. "Detects Threats 10x Faster Than Splunk"
   - Specific, comparable, provable

2. "$999/Month vs. $50,000/Year"
   - Concrete, relatable numbers

3. "Deploy in 30 Minutes, Not 3 Months"
   - Specific, measurable benefit

4. "AI Reduces False Positives by 70%"
   - Quantified improvement

5. "Works with Your Existing Tools"
   - Concrete, non-disruptive
